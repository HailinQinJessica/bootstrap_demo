KEEP

What is KEEP?

Knowledge & Education Exchange Platform

  • KEEP is a project developed and initiated by The Chinese University of Hong Kong, and funded by the University Grant Committee.
  • Working alongside our sister institutions, KEEP is committed to bringing you quality content, resourceful information, innovative learning tools and powerful applications from the education landscape.

Why KEEP?

Anyone,Anytime,Anywhere

At KEEP, the Knowledge & Education Exchange platform, we thrive to provide the best eLearning experience in Hong Kong and beyond.

Our aim is to build a central-point, one-stop education cloud. As a KEEP user, you can access the full array of education resources, particularly those developed by educators in Hong Kong. Showcase, share and search educational content, tools, applications and services.

What's In It For You?

  • Educators: In-depth analytical reports, innovative and sound pedagogies, knowledge exchange and sharing.
  • Learners: A personalized learning path, meaningful search results, and an enhanced learning experience.
  • All KEEP participants: Community building, collaborating, and feedback on tools, courses, applications.
